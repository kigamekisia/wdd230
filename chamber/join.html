Member Information

Membership Levels

Non-Profit Membership

This membership level is for non profit organizations and it is free with no membership fees

Bronze Membership

The Bronze level is for small and micro enterprises. The membership fees is $10. You will be able to recieve numerous trainings to grow your business.

Silver Membership

Silver level is for medium level businesses. The membership fees is $20. You will recieve capacity building and your business will be advertised to provide linkages to the market.

Gold Membership

Gold level is for large businesses especially manufacturing. The membership fees is $40. You will be linked directly to the consumers to buy your products both locally and internationally, besides being provided with advertisement and capacity building.
