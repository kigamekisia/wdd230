News Release

Vihiga Chamber of Commerce has signed a Memorandum of understanding with the County Government of Vihiga which will see 70% of all county Government procurements done locally. This move is set to see members of the chamber benefit greatly and reduce capital flights witnessed in most counties. The Chamber wishes to extend gratitude to the Govenor for embracing local businesses. Most businesses both micro and large corporates will benefit from this agreement.
